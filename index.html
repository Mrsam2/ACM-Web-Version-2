Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a center of excellence in Computer Engineering education and research, producing competent professionals with social responsibility and ethical values.

Mission

  • To impart quality education through effective teaching-learning processes.
  • To promote research and innovation in emerging technologies.
  • To foster industry-academia collaboration for professional development.
  • To inculcate ethical values and leadership qualities among students.
