About the Chapter

Our Story

The St. Vincent Pallotti CE ACM Student Chapter was established in 2018 with the vision of creating a vibrant community of computer engineering students passionate about technology and innovation. Since its inception, the chapter has been dedicated to fostering technical knowledge, promoting collaboration, and encouraging research among students.

What We Aim To Do

Our chapter aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements by:

  • Organizing workshops, seminars, and hands-on sessions on cutting-edge technologies
  • Facilitating collaboration between students on innovative projects
  • Providing a platform for students to showcase their technical skills
  • Creating networking opportunities with industry professionals and alumni
  • Encouraging participation in technical competitions and hackathons

Who Can Join

The ACM Student Chapter is open to:

  • All students from the Department of Computer Engineering
  • Students from other engineering departments with an interest in computing
  • Faculty members who wish to mentor and guide student activities

We welcome enthusiasts at all skill levels, from beginners to advanced, as our goal is to create an inclusive learning environment where everyone can grow and contribute.
